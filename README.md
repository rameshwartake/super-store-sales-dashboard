# 📊 Super Store Sales Dashboard
“Interactive Super Store Dashboard with 15-Day Sales Forecast”

An interactive **Super Store Sales Dashboard** built to analyze sales performance, customer segments, regional trends, shipping modes, and future sales predictions.  
The project also includes a **15-day sales forecast** to help predict short-term trends and support **data-driven business decisions**.

---

## 📊 Key Metrics
- Total Orders: 5K  
- Total Sales: 2.25M 💰  
- Total Profit: 563K 📈  
- Average Delivery Time: 4 Days ⏱️  

---

## ✨ Key Insights
- Consumer Segment contributes the highest share of sales (~50%)  
- Technology is the top revenue-generating category, followed by Furniture and Office Supplies  
- Phones and Chairs are the highest-selling sub-categories  
- Standard Class is the most preferred shipping mode  
- California, New York, and Texas are the top sales-contributing states  
- Sales increase significantly in Q3–Q4, especially Nov–Dec  
- 15-Day Sales Forecast helps predict short-term trends and supports proactive planning
